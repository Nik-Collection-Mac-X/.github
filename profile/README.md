## Overview

Nik Collection Advanced is a suite of professional photo editing plugins from DxO that provides specialized creative effects including HDR processing, black and white conversion, analog film emulation, selective detail enhancement, color stylization, and perspective correction through eight dedicated plugins that integrate with Photoshop, Lightroom, and other photo editors. Each plugin focuses on a specific creative domain with depth that general-purpose editors cannot match.

Color Efex Pro provides over 50 creative filters and effects with control point technology that applies adjustments selectively to specific image regions without creating masks. The filters cover everything from subtle enhancement to dramatic stylization, and they combine to create custom looks. The control point system makes targeted adjustments intuitive and fast.

Silver Efex Pro handles black and white conversion with the depth and nuance that monochrome photography demands, providing film emulation, toning, grain simulation, and zone-based exposure control that produce black and white images with the character of specific analog film stocks. The conversion quality is widely regarded as the best available.

HDR Efex Pro merges bracketed exposures into high dynamic range images with tone mapping controls that balance realism with creative impact. The merge engine handles alignment, deghosting, and tone mapping in a guided workflow. Dfine provides noise reduction, Sharpener Pro handles output sharpening, Viveza provides selective color and tonality adjustment, and Perspective Efex corrects lens distortion and perspective.

For photographers who want the creative depth that specialized tools provide for specific photographic challenges — HDR, black and white, film looks, detail enhancement — Nik Collection Advanced is the plugin suite that adds professional creative capability to any photo editor.

## Performance

Nik Collection balances performance with system resource usage intelligently. Operations are handled in the background, keeping your system responsive for everything else. Efficient memory management and smart caching reduce overhead during extended work sessions.

Apple Silicon acceleration is fully utilized on M-series chips, delivering noticeably faster performance. On Intel machines, hardware acceleration is used wherever available.
